About Me

Hello! I'm Rico Fiqry Rhahimy, a passionate Informatics Student at Universitas Ahmad Dahlan in Yogyakarta, Indonesia. With a strong foundation in computer science and a keen interest in web development, I specialize in creating innovative digital solutions that bridge technology and user needs.

Born and raised in Yogyakarta, I've always been fascinated by how technology can solve real-world problems. My academic journey has equipped me with solid knowledge in programming, algorithms, and software engineering. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Informatics from UAD, where I graduated with honors.

My journey in tech began during my high school years when I first learned to code. Since then, I've dedicated myself to continuous learning, exploring various programming languages, frameworks, and tools. I believe in the power of collaboration and open-source contributions to drive innovation.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy cycling through the scenic streets of Yogyakarta, hiking in nearby mountains, and reading books on technology and personal development. I'm also an avid gamer and love participating in hackathons and tech meetups.

I'm always eager to take on new challenges, collaborate on exciting projects, and contribute to the tech community. Whether it's building responsive websites, developing mobile apps, or exploring emerging technologies like AI and blockchain, I'm ready to make an impact. Let's connect and build something amazing together!
